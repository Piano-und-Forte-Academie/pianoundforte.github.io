2025 Berlin Piano Concerto Winter Camp

An eight-day winter master class on the piano concerto, with live rehearsal alongside a professional ensemble at the Berlin International Classical Music Festival.

Dates
January 31 – February 7, 2025
Cities
Berlin
Faculty
International guest faculty
Winter Camp
Past
View photo album

The piano concerto master class of the 2025 Berlin International Classical Music Festival offered young talents a rare platform to learn under internationally renowned mentors — personalised one-to-one guidance together with the experience of live rehearsal alongside a professional orchestra.

For whom

  • Students entering, or already studying at, professional music conservatories.
  • Future students who want an early taste of study life in Germany and Europe.
  • Performers preparing for competitions at home and abroad, eager to break new ground.

Course highlights

  • Direct guidance from mentors drawn from leading conservatories worldwide.
  • Insight into the standards of international competition juries.
  • A practical look at music theory for German conservatory entrance exams.
  • European music history through the lens of the development of instruments.

Programme

Two one-to-one master classes with piano accompaniment in place of orchestra, one rehearsal with the ensemble, and performances at the opening and closing ceremonies — eight days in Berlin in the depths of winter.
