2026 Berlin Study Camp

A three-week immersive study camp in Berlin — five master classes, European music history, concerts and intensive German, for students aiming at study abroad and international competitions.

Dates
July 22 – August 12, 2026
Cities
Berlin
Faculty
Universität der Künste Berlin faculty · Hochschule für Musik Hanns Eisler Berlin faculty
Summer Camp
Upcoming

Part of the 4th Berlin International Music Festival, the Study Camp is a three-week, fully immersive programme for music learners who want to raise their performance level and for students preparing for overseas study, professional exams and international competitions.

  • Disciplines: piano, violin, cello.
  • Dates: 22 July – 12 August 2026, Berlin.

Programme

  • 5 one-to-one master classes over three weeks, with each student receiving individual guidance from at least two professors from different institutions. Repertoire: two works from different periods and styles.
  • 2 European music history sessions — instrument history at the Berlin Musical Instrument Museum and music literature at the Berlin State Library, where students can study composers’ manuscripts, letters, diaries and full orchestral scores.
  • 2 concerts — an opening concert and a closing concert.
  • ~40 intensive German lessons over three weeks, focused on listening and speaking in a real German-language environment.

Living and learning in Berlin

Guided throughout by a dedicated companion, students immerse themselves in everyday life in Germany — getting to know the neighbourhood and supermarkets, mastering public transport, and experiencing authentic local culture.
